About me

I am a PhD student in the Scool team (previously SequeL) at Inria Lille (France) under the supervision of Debabrota Basu and Philippe Preux. I am exploring privacy-preserving machine learning in sequential learning. Prior to my PhD, I graduated from École Polytechnique and ENS Paris-Saclay.
